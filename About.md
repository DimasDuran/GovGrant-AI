**GovGrant AI** is an autonomous AI agent designed to simplify and automate technical compliance for U.S. federal grant applications (SBIR/STTR). It leverages multimodal Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to understand complex application documents and an action engine to validate budgets, eligibility requirements, and compliance criteria. By combining advanced document layout parsing with agentic AI frameworks, GovGrant AI acts as a specialized technical grant consultant, helping applicants navigate the bureaucratic requirements of agencies such as the DoD, NIH, and NSF.
